# Arc by 3 Points

> **In one sentence:** Draws a circular arc through three clicked points,
> computing the unique circle that passes through all three.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Menu:** Draft → Drafting → Arc Tools → Arc by 3 Points  
**Command:** `Draft_Arc_3Points`

FreeCAD computes the unique circle passing through three non-collinear points
and shows the arc between the first and third points. Faster than the
centre-radius-angle method when you know where the arc must pass.

---

## When to use

- Fitting an arc through three known survey or layout points.
- Matching an arc to three snapped geometry positions.
- When the centre and radius are not known but the pass-through points are.
