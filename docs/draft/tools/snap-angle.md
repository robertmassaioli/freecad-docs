# Snap Angle

> **In one sentence:** Snaps to points at 45° increments around a circle or
> arc (0°, 45°, 90°, 135°, 180°, 225°, 270°, 315°).

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Toolbar:** Draft Snap  
**Command:** `Draft_Snap_Angle`

**Indicator:** Yellow dot on the arc at 45° positions.

Snaps to the quarter-points and eighth-points of a circle. Useful for
placing geometry at exact cardinal and diagonal positions.
