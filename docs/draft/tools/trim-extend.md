# Trim / Extend

> **In one sentence:** Trims a wire back to a boundary edge or extends it to
> meet a boundary edge — the same tool handles both operations.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Menu:** Draft → Modification → Trim / Extend  
**Command:** `Draft_Trimex`

FreeCAD detects which operation is needed based on whether the boundary is
before or beyond the clicked point.

---

## Step-by-step

1. Activate **Draft → Modification → Trim / Extend**.
2. Click on the **wire to trim or extend** — click the part to keep for
   trim, or click the end to extend.
3. Click on the **boundary edge** to trim/extend to.
