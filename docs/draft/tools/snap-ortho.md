# Snap Ortho

> **In one sentence:** Snaps the cursor to positions at 45° intervals from
> the previous drawn point — enforcing horizontal, vertical, or 45° diagonal lines.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Toolbar:** Draft Snap  
**Command:** `Draft_Snap_Ortho`

**Indicator:** Dashed yellow line at the ortho direction from the last point.

When the cursor is near an ortho direction (0°, 45°, 90°, 135°, 180°, 225°,
270°, 315°), it locks to that angle. Snap Ortho enforces straight axis-aligned
or 45° diagonal segments.
