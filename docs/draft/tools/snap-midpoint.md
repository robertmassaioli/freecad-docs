# Snap Midpoint

> **In one sentence:** Snaps the cursor to the midpoint of any edge — exactly
> halfway along the edge.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Toolbar:** Draft Snap  
**Command:** `Draft_Snap_Midpoint`

**Indicator:** Yellow triangle at the midpoint.

Useful for centring objects, placing reference lines at bisectors, and
aligning to the middle of walls or structural members.
