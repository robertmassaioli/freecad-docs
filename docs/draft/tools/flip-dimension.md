# Flip Dimension

> **In one sentence:** Flips the text of a selected Draft Dimension to the
> other side of the dimension line.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Menu:** Draft → Modification → Flip Dimension  
**Command:** `Draft_FlipDimension`

Useful when the auto-placed dimension text overlaps with other geometry
or is on the wrong side for clarity in the drawing.
