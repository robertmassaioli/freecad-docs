# Snap Center

> **In one sentence:** Snaps to the centre of a circle or arc, the centroid
> of a planar face, or the centre of a sphere, cylinder, or cone face.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** Draft  
**Toolbar:** Draft Snap  
**Command:** `Draft_Snap_Center`

**Indicator:** Yellow circle cross-hair.

---

## What it snaps to

- Centre of a circle or arc
- Centre of a sphere, cylinder, or cone face
- Centroid of a planar face
- Midpoint of an edge or wire (when no other centre applies)
