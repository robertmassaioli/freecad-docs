# Insert Radius Dimension

> **In one sentence:** Measures the radius of an arc or circle, placing the
> dimension line from centre to edge with an **R** prefix.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** TechDraw  
**Menu:** TechDraw → Dimensions → Insert Radius Dimension

The dimension line starts at the arc centre and ends at the arc with an
arrowhead pointing outward. The value is prefixed with **R**.

---

## Step-by-step

1. Click a **circular arc or full circle** edge in a view.
2. Choose **TechDraw → Dimensions → Insert Radius Dimension**.
3. Click to place the dimension text.
