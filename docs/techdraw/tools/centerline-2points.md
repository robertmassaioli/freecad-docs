# Add Centerline Between 2 Points

> **In one sentence:** Draws a centerline between two selected vertices —
> for centerlines defined by two geometric points rather than two edges.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** TechDraw  
**Menu:** TechDraw → Add Lines → Add Centerline Between 2 Points

Use this when the centerline axis is defined by two geometric points rather
than by two edges or a face.

---

## Step-by-step

1. Ctrl-click two vertices in a view.
2. Choose **TechDraw → Add Lines → Add Centerline Between 2 Points**.
3. The centerline appears in long-dash center-line ISO style.
