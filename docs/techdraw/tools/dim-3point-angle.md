# Insert 3-Point Angle Dimension

> **In one sentence:** Measures the angle at a vertex defined by three selected
> vertices — for angles that cannot be defined by two edges alone.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** TechDraw  
**Menu:** TechDraw → Dimensions → Insert 3-Point Angle Dimension

The angle is measured from the line (vertex1 → vertex2) to the line
(vertex2 → vertex3), where vertex2 is the apex.

---

## When to use it

Use this instead of the two-edge angle dimension when:
- The angle is defined by three geometric points, not two edges.
- Measuring the angle of a hole pattern measured from a central point.
- The two edges do not actually intersect in the view.

---

## Step-by-step

1. Click **vertex1** in a view.
2. Ctrl-click **vertex2** (the apex).
3. Ctrl-click **vertex3**.
4. Choose **TechDraw → Dimensions → Insert 3-Point Angle Dimension**.
5. Click to place the dimension arc and text.
