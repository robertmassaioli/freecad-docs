# Workbenches

FreeCAD organises its tools into **workbenches** — focused environments each
covering a distinct domain of work. Choose a workbench below to explore its
tools and concepts. All documentation is accurate for FreeCAD 1.1.

---

## Core Modelling

The workbenches most users spend most of their time in.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-cube-outline:{ .lg .middle } __Part Design__

    ---

    Parametric solid modelling with the Body feature tree — Pad, Pocket,
    Hole, Loft, and every dress-up and transformation operation.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Part Design](../part-design/index.md)

-   :material-pencil-ruler:{ .lg .middle } __Sketcher__

    ---

    Fully constrained 2-D profiles that drive Part Design features.
    Geometric and dimensional constraints, B-splines, external geometry.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Sketcher](../sketcher/index.md)

-   :material-shape-outline:{ .lg .middle } __Part__

    ---

    Lower-level Boolean operations, primitives, sweeps, and lofts directly
    on OCCT solids — without the feature-tree model.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Part](../part/index.md)

-   :material-table:{ .lg .middle } __Spreadsheet__

    ---

    Parametric master-model spreadsheets: cell aliases, formula expressions,
    and driving model dimensions from a spreadsheet.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Spreadsheet](../spreadsheet/index.md)

</div>

## Engineering & Analysis

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-function-variant:{ .lg .middle } __FEM__

    ---

    Finite element analysis — mesh generation, materials, mechanical and
    thermal loads, CalculiX/Elmer solvers, and post-processing.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore FEM](../fem/index.md)

-   :material-link-variant:{ .lg .middle } __Assembly__

    ---

    Multi-body assembly with kinematic, geometric, and coupled joints.
    Exploded views, simulation, and bill of materials.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Assembly](../assembly/index.md)

-   :material-ruler:{ .lg .middle } __Measure__

    ---

    Linear, angular, and radius measurements directly on 3-D geometry.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Measure](../measure/index.md)

-   :material-magnify:{ .lg .middle } __Inspection__

    ---

    Visual inspection and element-level interrogation of solid geometry.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Inspection](../inspection/index.md)

</div>

## Drawing & Documentation

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-drawing:{ .lg .middle } __TechDraw__

    ---

    2-D engineering drawings from 3-D models — orthographic views,
    dimensions, annotations, hatching, and the extension pack.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore TechDraw](../techdraw/index.md)

-   :material-vector-line:{ .lg .middle } __Draft__

    ---

    2-D freehand drawing, annotations, arrays, and snapping tools.
    Geometry lives in 3-D space on a configurable working plane.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Draft](../draft/index.md)

-   :material-city:{ .lg .middle } __BIM__

    ---

    Building Information Modelling — IFC elements, structure hierarchy,
    annotations, and IFC file management.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore BIM](../bim/index.md)

</div>

## Advanced & Specialist

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-cog:{ .lg .middle } __CAM__

    ---

    Computer-aided manufacturing — job setup, machining operations,
    dress-ups, and tool bit libraries for CNC workflows.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore CAM](../cam/index.md)

-   :material-wave:{ .lg .middle } __Surface__

    ---

    NURBS surface modelling — filling, sections, blend curves, face
    extension, and curve-on-mesh.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Surface](../surface/index.md)

-   :material-grid:{ .lg .middle } __Mesh__

    ---

    Import, repair, analyse, and convert STL and other mesh formats.
    The gateway for 3-D scan and print file workflows.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Mesh](../mesh/index.md)

-   :material-chart-bell-curve:{ .lg .middle } __Curves Workbench__

    ---

    Advanced curve and surface tools — interpolation curves, parametric
    surfaces, and analysis utilities beyond the built-in workbenches.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Curves Workbench](../curves-wb/index.md)

-   :material-rotate-3d:{ .lg .middle } __Reverse Engineering__

    ---

    Point cloud and mesh reconstruction into surface geometry —
    segmentation, approximation, and surface fitting.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Reverse Engineering](../reverse-engineering/index.md)

-   :material-robot:{ .lg .middle } __Robot__

    ---

    Industrial robot arm simulation and trajectory planning.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Robot](../robot/index.md)

-   :material-dots-grid:{ .lg .middle } __Points__

    ---

    Point cloud import, display, and basic manipulation.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore Points](../points/index.md)

-   :material-code-braces:{ .lg .middle } __OpenSCAD__

    ---

    Import and interact with OpenSCAD geometry inside FreeCAD, and
    convert between OpenSCAD and FreeCAD representations.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Explore OpenSCAD](../openscad/index.md)

</div>
