# Equation: Flux

> **In one sentence:** Elmer post-processing equation that computes heat flux
> or electric flux from the gradient of a scalar field solved by another equation.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** FEM  
**Menu:** FEM → Solve → Elmer Equations → Flux

Computes the flux (heat flux or electric flux) from the gradient of a scalar
field already solved by another equation. Used as a post-processing step after
solving the [Heat Equation](equation-heat.md) or [Electrostatic Equation](equation-electrostatic.md).
