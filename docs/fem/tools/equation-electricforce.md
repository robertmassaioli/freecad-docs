# Equation: Electricforce

> **In one sentence:** Elmer equation that computes the electrostatic body
> force on dielectric or conducting bodies — used for electro-mechanical coupling.

---

## Overview

**Workbench:** FEM  
**Menu:** FEM → Solve → Elmer Equations → Electricforce

Computes the electrostatic body force on dielectric or conducting bodies due
to the electric field. Typically coupled with the [Electrostatic Equation](equation-electrostatic.md)
and [Elasticity Equation](equation-elasticity.md) to compute
electrostatically-induced deformation (electro-mechanical coupling).
